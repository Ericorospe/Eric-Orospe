ABOUT ME







Hello, I'm Eric Fernando Orospe Estrada, an industrial and graphic designer deeply passionate about 3D modeling. I relish crafting diverse 3D models, bringing them to life through printing. My strong interest in corporate identity design has led me to collaborate with three brands on original creations and redesigns. A graduate of Anáhuac University of Querétaro, I find fulfillment in aiding others, employing design and 3D modeling as my primary tools.
