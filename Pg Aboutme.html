ABOUT ME







Hello, my name is Eric Fernando Orospe Estrada. I'm an industrial and graphic designer with a deep passion for 3D modeling. I enjoy creating a variety of 3D models and bringing them to life through 3D printing. Additionally, I have a keen interest in corporate identity design, having worked with three brands on both original creations and redesigns. I graduated from the Anáhuac University of Querétaro, and I find fulfillment in helping others, utilizing design and 3D modeling as my primary tools.
